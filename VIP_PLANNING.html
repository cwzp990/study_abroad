1.All-round service process

1.Students fill in the personal information material form and set up study abroad files
2.Register to open the VIP right, start the two-way selection process, and enjoy the overseas study planning of yingyixuan series

2.Background assessment of studying abroad

1. After the two-way selection is successful, the VIP service team will be matched, and the experienced domestic application planning tutors, plus tutors from overseas famous universities with the same specialty, and also former admissions officers will help
2. One-to-one in-depth communication of industry cutting-edge information and the latest trends from colleges and universities to further clarify the direction of study-abroad planning

3.Make a growth plan

1. Rationally plan the course selection and maintain a high academic level
2. Simulate and analyze the appropriate examinations for studying abroad, and make a comprehensive learning plan; according to the children's specialty; make a comprehensive training plan for English, American and sports students
3. Follow up the implementation of the application plan in time and make adjustments according to the actual situation

4.Internship / research promotion

1. Weekly and monthly regular supervision to tap students' academic and other potential
2. According to the actual ability of the college, guide and sign up for the global related disciplines competition
3. Customize career planning and guidance services according to students' wishes
4. Matching related research and practical projects

5.Selection and determination of schools

1. Multi dimensional analysis of college professional information
2. Dual tutors analyze the advantages and disadvantages of the school
3. Encourage and help students to achieve their dream school

6.Creative writing of documents

1. Dual tutors (application planning tutor and overseas document tutor) brainstorm the document content and framework with students
2. Refine the logic and details of the document
3. After the first draft of the document, the team will conduct internal audit and put forward suggestions for revision. No limit to the number of revisions
4. Parents and students can review the documents and put forward suggestions for revision. No limit to the number of revisions
5. The director team of yingyixuan research department reviews the documents, puts forward suggestions for revision and makes amendments
6. Foreign grammar tutors / admissions officers modify and polish the documents
7. Modify the documents according to the requirements and styles of different schools
8. Finalization of documents
